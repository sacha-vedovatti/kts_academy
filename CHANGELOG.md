# Update [2.1.0] - 2026-03-06

- **[ADD] Legendaries**: Added Generation 1 legendaries in wild:
    - Articuno
    - Zapdos
    - Moltres
    - Mewtwo _(texture is bugged, will be fixed in a future update)_

- **[UPDATE] Mods**:
  - **[ADD] Cobblemon Exp Lock**
  - **[ADD] Chunk Loader**: Add "Single Chunk Loader" (`1x1`) and "Basic Chunk Loader" (`3x3`) items.
  - **[UPDATE] Cobblemon**: Updated to version `1.7.3`.
  - **[UPDATE] Raids**: Updated to version `0.8.1`.
  - **[REMOVE] Tim Core & Capture XP**: Temporarily removed due to a bug.

- **[UPDATE] Raids**:
  - **[FIX] Tier 6 Levels**: Pokémon levels in Tier 6 raids have been set to 100 instead of 75. Rewards level has been set to 100 as well.
  - **[BUFF] Raid Rewards**: Deleted the "Decrease IV Candy" reward.
  - **[NERF] Tier 6 Rate**: Tier 6 raids weight have been set to 2.5 instead of 5.
  - **[NERF] Max Players**: Max players in raids has been set to 3 instead of 4.

- **[UPDATE] Quests Rewards**:
  - **[ADD] Rewards**: added new rare rewards: Radiant Fossil, Shiny Fossil, Max IV Fossil, HA Fossil
  - **[BUFF] Rewards**: Increased Rare Candy, Poke Ball and Ultra Ball rewards.
  - **[FIX] Chat Message**: fixed a bug where the name of the reward was named "Air" instead of the reward name.
  - **[REMOVE] Master Ball Reward**: removed Master Ball reward from quests.

- **[BUFF] Poke Snacks**: Restored default effects. Increased maximum Pokémon per chunk from 2 to 5.
- **[BUFF] Radiant Fossil**: Pokémon obtained from Radiant Fossil will now have a guaranteed 3 perfect IVs.
- **[BUFF] Gyms Rewards**: Replaced "Gym Cache" tier from rare to epic for tier 9 and tier 10.
- **[NERF] Money Rewards**: Removed money rewards from Pokemon defeated and reduced money rewards from captured.
- **[FIX] Client Crash**: Fixed a crash when opening a chest too quickly after a teleportation (chest not loaded).
- **[ADD] Farmland Protection**: Added farmland protection to prevent trampling and breaking crops.
- **[ADD] Command PC**: added a command to access your PC from anywhere: `/pc`.
- **[UPDATE] Wiki**: updated link & content.
